<p><p>The car tents market is classified into three main types: soft shell, hard shell, and hybrid shell. Soft shell tents are lightweight and easy to pack, providing flexibility but often less durability. Hard shell tents offer robust protection and are quicker to set up, featuring a rigid structure, which enhances longevity. Hybrid shell tents combine elements of both, offering a balance between portability and strength. Each type caters to different outdoor enthusiasts’ needs and preferences, influencing their buying decisions.</p></p>
