<p><strong>The Half Couplings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw Thread Type</li><li>Socket Welding Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The half couplings market can be categorized into two main types: screw thread type and socket welding type. Screw thread half couplings utilize threaded connections to join pipes, offering ease of assembly and disassembly, making them suitable for applications requiring frequent maintenance. In contrast, socket welding half couplings involve inserting a pipe into a coupling socket, providing a strong and permanent joint ideal for high-pressure environments. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including oil and gas, chemical, and construction.</p></p>
