<p><ul><li>Isotonic Sea Water Nasal Spray</li><li>Hypertonic Sea Water Nasal Spray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sea water nasal spray market is primarily divided into two types: isotonic and hypertonic formulations. Isotonic sea water nasal sprays have a similar salt concentration to the body, providing gentle hydration and soothing nasal passages, making them ideal for daily use. Hypertonic sea water nasal sprays contain a higher salt concentration, promoting decongestion by drawing excess fluid from nasal tissues, effectively alleviating symptoms of sinus congestion. Both types cater to varying user needs for nasal care and respiratory health.</p></p>
