<p><p>The abdominal stent graft market is categorized into two primary types: integrated and split. Integrated stent grafts combine the stent and graft components into a single device, providing a streamlined solution for vascular repairs. This design enhances stability and reduces the risk of complications. Conversely, split stent grafts involve separate stent and graft components, allowing for more flexibility in treatment options and customization. Both types are crucial for addressing abdominal aortic aneurysms and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
