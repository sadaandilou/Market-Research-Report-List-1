<p><p>The silicone sealant for PV modules market is primarily divided into two types: one-component and two-component sealants. One-component sealants are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, offering convenience and ease of application. Two-component sealants, on the other hand, require mixing before application, providing enhanced strength and durability. Both types are essential for ensuring optimal adhesion and weather resistance in photovoltaic installations, protecting against environmental factors while enhancing the longevity of solar panels.</p></p>
