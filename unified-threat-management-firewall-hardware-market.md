<p><p>Unified Threat Management (UTM) firewalls offer different operating modes to cater to various network needs. </p><p>In Routing Working Mode, the UTM functions as a router, managing traffic between different networks while applying security rules. </p><p>Transparent Working Mode allows it to act as a bridge, securing traffic without altering IP addresses, making it easier to integrate into existing networks. </p><p>Mixed Working Mode combines both routing and transparent capabilities, providing flexibility in diverse environments, allowing for optimized security and traffic management.</p></p>
