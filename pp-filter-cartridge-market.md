<p><ul><li>PP Melt Blown Filter Cartridges</li><li>PP String Wound Filter Cartridges</li><li>PP Spun Filter Cartridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PP filter cartridge market encompasses several types, including PP melt blown, PP string wound, and PP spun filter cartridges. PP melt blown cartridges are produced by melting polypropylene and blowing it into fine fibers, resulting in excellent filtration efficiency and dirt-holding capacity. PP string wound cartridges feature a winding of polypropylene yarn around a core, providing high dirt retention and structural integrity. PP spun cartridges utilize a process that resembles melt blowing but creates a uniform thickness, ideal for various filtration applications.</p></p>
