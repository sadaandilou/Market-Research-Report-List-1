<p><p>Automotive floor insulation encompasses three primary market types: heat insulation, sound insulation, and noise insulation. Heat insulation protects the cabin from external temperatures, improving comfort and energy efficiency. Sound insulation focuses on enhancing acoustic comfort by minimizing unwanted vibrations and sounds from the road. Noise insulation, while often overlapping with sound insulation, specifically targets resonance and disturbances within the vehicle to create a quieter driving experience. Together, these insulation types contribute to overall vehicle performance and passenger satisfaction.</p></p>
