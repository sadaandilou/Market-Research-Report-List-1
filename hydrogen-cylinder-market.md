<p><p>The hydrogen cylinder market consists of five main types based on their construction materials and designs. Type I cylinders are made of a solid steel structure, offering durability but heavier weight. Type II cylinders combine steel and a composite overwrap, reducing weight. Type III cylinders use an aluminum liner with composite wrap, providing better weight savings. Type IV cylinders feature a polymer liner fully wrapped in composite materials, ensuring maximum lightweight and increased safety. Type V encompasses advanced designs, integrating innovative materials for optimized performance.</p></p>
