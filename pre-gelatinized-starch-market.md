<p><p>Pre-gelatinized starch is a modified form of starch that has been treated through a process of gelatinization and drying, allowing it to dissolve in cold water without the need for cooking. This property makes it an essential ingredient in various food products, including sauces, instant meals, and baked goods, as it enhances texture and stability. Additionally, pre-gelatinized starch finds applications in the pharmaceutical and cosmetic industries due to its binding and thickening properties.</p><p>The Pre-gelatinized Starch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.00% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing demand for convenience foods, as consumers seek quick and easy meal solutions. Furthermore, the rise in health consciousness has led to a preference for gluten-free and clean-label products, propelling the use of alternative starches. Innovations in food processing technologies and the expanding use of pre-gelatinized starch in non-food applications are also key trends shaping the market.
