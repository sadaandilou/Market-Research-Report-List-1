<p><p>The home solar energy storage battery market offers two main types: AC-coupled and DC-coupled batteries. AC-coupled solar batteries connect to the inverter and allow for easy integration with existing solar systems, making them ideal for retrofitting. In contrast, DC-coupled batteries connect directly to the solar panels, maximizing efficiency and energy use by storing power before it reaches the inverter. Each type has unique benefits, making them suitable for various installation needs and energy management strategies in residential settings.</p></p>
