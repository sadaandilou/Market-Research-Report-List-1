<p><ul><li>Roller Type</li><li>Ball Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rolling guide markets are categorized into two main types: roller type and ball type. The roller type utilizes cylindrical rollers to facilitate smooth linear movement and support heavy loads, making it ideal for applications requiring stability and durability. In contrast, the ball type employs spherical balls for movement, offering lower friction and higher speeds but with a reduced load capacity. Both types are essential in various industries, providing efficient solutions for guiding mechanisms in machinery and equipment.</p></p>
