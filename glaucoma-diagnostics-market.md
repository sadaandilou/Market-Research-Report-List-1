<p><p>The Glaucoma Diagnostics Market is segmented into several types based on the variations of the disease. Open Angle Glaucoma, the most common form, occurs with gradual vision loss. Low Tension Glaucoma features optic nerve damage despite normal intraocular pressure. Pigmentary Glaucoma is linked to pigment dispersion in the eye. Closed Angle Glaucoma arises from a blockage in the drainage angle, leading to sudden symptoms. Additionally, the market includes various other types not specified above, encompassing miscellaneous glaucoma conditions.</p></p>
