<p><p>A sputter coater is a device used to deposit thin films of material onto substrates, typically for applications in microscopy, semiconductor manufacturing, and optics. This process is essential for enhancing the conductivity of non-conductive samples in electron microscopy and improving the performance of electronic devices.</p><p>The Sputter Coater Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period.
