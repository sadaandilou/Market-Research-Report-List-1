<p><p>The Patrol ACV market comprises two primary types: skirted air-cushion vehicles and sidewall air-cushion vehicles. Skirted air-cushion vehicles utilize a flexible skirt to create a cushion of air beneath, enhancing lift and maneuverability, making them ideal for varied terrains. In contrast, sidewall air-cushion vehicles have vertical sidewalls, providing stability and allowing for more robust operations in rough environments. Both types are used in military and law enforcement applications for reconnaissance, transport, and rapid response in diverse landscapes.</p></p>
