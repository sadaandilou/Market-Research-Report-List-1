<p><p>The smart speaker chipset market can be categorized into two main types: networked and non-networkable. Networked chipsets enable devices to connect to the internet, facilitating streaming, cloud services, and smart home integration, enhancing user experience through voice commands and online interactions. While networked chipsets cater to advanced smart speakers, non-networkable options target budget-conscious consumers with simple audio needs.</p></p>
