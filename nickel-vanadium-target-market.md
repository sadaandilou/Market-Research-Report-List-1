<p><p>Nickel vanadium targets are used in various applications, primarily in the production of thin films for electronics. The market is divided into two main types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat and provide uniform deposition but are limited by their sputtering efficiency. In contrast, rotating targets enhance sputtering performance by allowing continuous motion, thus offering better material utilization and increased deposition rates. Each type serves specific applications depending on the required coating quality and production efficiency.</p></p>
