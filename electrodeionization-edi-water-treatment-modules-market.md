<p><ul><li>Plate-and-Frame EDI Modules</li><li>Spiral-Wound EDI Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrodeionization (EDI) water treatment modules are primarily categorized into two types: Plate-and-Frame EDI Modules and Spiral-Wound EDI Modules. Plate-and-Frame modules consist of flat plates arranged in a frame, allowing for efficient ion exchange and improved flow rates, making them suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, Spiral-Wound modules utilize a compact, coiled design that enhances surface area and efficiency, ideal for smaller applications with space constraints. Both types effectively produce high-purity water by removing ions through electrodialysis.</p></p>
