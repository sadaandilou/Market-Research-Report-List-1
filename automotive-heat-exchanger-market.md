<p><ul><li>HVAC Thermal Management</li><li>Powertrain Thermal Management</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive heat exchanger market comprises two primary types: HVAC thermal management and powertrain thermal management. HVAC thermal management focuses on regulating cabin temperature to enhance passenger comfort, utilizing heat exchangers to effectively transfer heat between the vehicle's interior and the external environment. In contrast, powertrain thermal management is concerned with maintaining optimal operating temperatures for engine and transmission systems, ensuring efficiency and performance. Both types are crucial for improving energy efficiency and overall vehicle performance in modern automobiles.</p></p>
