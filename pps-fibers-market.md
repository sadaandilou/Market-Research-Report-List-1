<p><p>The PPS fibers market can be categorized into two main types: PPS filaments and PPS staple fibers. PPS filaments are continuous strands used in applications requiring high strength and durability, such as textiles, automotive parts, and industrial products. In contrast, PPS staple fibers are short fibers used in nonwovens, insulation, and various composite materials, providing benefits like thermal resistance and lightweight properties. Both types are prized for their superior chemical resistance and thermal stability, making them suitable for demanding environments.</p></p>
