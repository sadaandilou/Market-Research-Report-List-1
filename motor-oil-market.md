<p><p>The motor oil market is divided into three main types: ordinary motor oil, semi-synthetic oil, and synthetic oil. Ordinary motor oil, derived from crude oil, provides basic engine protection and lubrication but may require more frequent changes. Semi-synthetic oil blends conventional oils with synthetic additives, offering improved performance and protection than ordinary oil. Synthetic oil, engineered from chemical compounds, delivers superior performance under extreme conditions, better temperature stability, and extended oil change intervals, making it ideal for high-performance and luxury vehicles.</p></p>
