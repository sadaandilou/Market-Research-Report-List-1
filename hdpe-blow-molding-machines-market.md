<p><p>The HDPE blow molding machines market is categorized into three primary types: </p><p>1. **Extrusion Blow Molding Machines**: These machines extrude molten HDPE into a mold, allowing for the production of hollow items like bottles. </p><p>2. **Injection Blow Molding Machines**: This method involves injecting HDPE into a pre-mold, followed by expansion into a final mold, ideal for high-precision parts. </p><p>3. **Others**: This segment includes hybrid or specialized machines that combine various technologies to meet specific production needs, enhancing versatility in applications.</p></p>
