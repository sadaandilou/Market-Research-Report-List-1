<p><p>In-Service Inspection Services encompass various techniques used to assess the condition of equipment during operation. Ultrasound Inspection utilizes sound waves to detect flaws in materials, ensuring structural integrity. Infrared Thermal Imaging Inspection identifies heat anomalies, revealing potential faults in electrical systems or machinery. Magnetic Particle Inspection employs magnetic fields to uncover surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials. Other methods may include eddy current testing, visual inspection, and radiographic evaluation, each contributing to effective maintenance and safety measures in various industries.</p></p>
