<p><ul><li>Resting System</li><li>Stress System</li><li>Holter Monitor System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrocardiograph (ECG) market is categorized into several types: Resting systems, Stress systems, and Holter monitor systems. Resting systems are stationary devices used to capture a snapshot of the heart's electrical activity at rest. Stress systems assess cardiac performance under exercise conditions, helping diagnose heart issues during physical stress. Holter monitors are portable ECG devices worn for extended periods, typically 24 to 48 hours, providing comprehensive insights into heart rhythms during daily activities. Each type plays a crucial role in cardiac diagnostics and management.</p></p>
