<p><ul><li>Fixed RFID Readers</li><li>Handheld RFID Readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) Reader market consists of two primary types: fixed RFID readers and handheld RFID readers. Fixed RFID readers are installed at specific locations, such as entry and exit points, to continuously monitor and track tagged items within a designated area, providing robust data collection for inventory and assets. In contrast, handheld RFID readers are portable devices that allow users to scan tags manually, offering flexibility for inventory checks, asset management, and mobile applications in various environments.</p></p>
