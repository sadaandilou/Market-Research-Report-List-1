<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic Train Operation</li><li>Driverless Train Operation</li><li>Unattended Train Operation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Train Operation Systems encompass various market types that enhance rail efficiency and safety. Semi-automatic Train Operation allows operators to assist in driving while automated systems handle control functions. Driverless Train Operation features fully automated trains, eliminating the need for human intervention in operations. Unattended Train Operation is similar but focuses on remote monitoring and control, ensuring safety without onboard staff. These systems aim to improve punctuality, reduce labor costs, and enhance overall passenger experience in urban transit networks.</p></p>
