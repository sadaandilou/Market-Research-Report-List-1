<p><p>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) transformers market is primarily divided into two types: Flyback and Forward transformers. Flyback transformers are widely used in low to moderate power applications, providing isolated power and enabling the integration of data and power transmission. In contrast, Forward transformers are suitable for higher power applications, offering efficiency and lower electromagnetic interference. Both types cater to the growing demand for PoE solutions in telecommunications, security systems, and smart buildings, enhancing connectivity and power delivery.</p></p>
