<p><p>Glass to Metal Sealing Technology involves joining glass and metal materials to create hermetic seals that protect sensitive electronic components. The market is primarily divided into two types: Glass-Metal Matching Sealing, which ensures thermal expansion compatibility between glass and metal to prevent stress and cracking during temperature changes, and Glass-Metal Compression Sealing, where glass is compressed against metal to form a tight seal. Both methods are crucial for applications in electronics, aerospace, and medical devices, ensuring durability and reliability.</p></p>
