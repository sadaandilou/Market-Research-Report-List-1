<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Sensor System</li><li>Pressure Sensor System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Rear Occupant Alert System market primarily features two types: Ultrasonic Sensor Systems and Pressure Sensor Systems. Ultrasonic Sensor Systems use sound waves to detect the presence of passengers in the rear seat, alerting the driver if someone is still inside when exiting the vehicle. Pressure Sensor Systems, on the other hand, rely on weight sensors within the seats to identify occupancy and provide similar alerts. Both systems enhance safety by preventing child or pet abandonment in vehicles.</p></p>
