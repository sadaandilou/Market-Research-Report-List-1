<p><strong>The Piezoresistive Force Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Mode FSR</li><li>Thru Mode FSR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoresistive force sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Shunt Mode Force Sensors (FSR) and Thru Mode FSR. Shunt Mode FSR operates by measuring changes in resistance as force is applied across a sensor element, typically exhibiting high sensitivity and a compact design. In contrast, Thru Mode FSR measures force by monitoring the resistance changes within a conductive path subjected to force, offering advantages in dynamic applications. Both types are widely used in various industries, including automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
