<p><p>Electric power transmission and distribution equipment encompasses various methods for delivering electricity from generation sources to end users. Overhead transmission involves power lines supported by towers or poles, offering cost-effective installation and maintenance but facing challenges like weather impacts and aesthetics. Conversely, underground transmission protects infrastructure from environmental factors and enhances visual appeal, though it entails higher installation costs and complexity. Both methods play crucial roles in ensuring reliable electricity distribution across urban and rural areas.</p></p>
