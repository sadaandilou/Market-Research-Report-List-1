<p><p>The CCTV video camera market comprises three main types: analog cameras, IP cameras, and hybrid cameras. Analog cameras transmit video signals over coaxial cables, offering a cost-effective solution for basic surveillance. IP cameras, on the other hand, utilize digital networking for higher resolution and remote accessibility, enabling advanced features like motion detection and cloud storage. Hybrid cameras combine both technologies, allowing users to integrate existing analog systems with new IP capabilities, providing flexibility and scalability for diverse security needs.</p></p>
