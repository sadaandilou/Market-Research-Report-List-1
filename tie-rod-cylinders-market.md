<p><p>Tie rod cylinders are versatile actuators used in various industrial applications. They primarily come in two types: hydraulic and pneumatic. Hydraulic tie rod cylinders utilize pressurized fluid for power, offering high force and precise control, making them suitable for heavy-duty tasks. Pneumatic tie rod cylinders, on the other hand, rely on compressed air, providing rapid movement and lower force output, ideal for lighter applications. Both types are designed with tie rods for structural integrity and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
