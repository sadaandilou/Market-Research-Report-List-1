<p><strong>The Automated Refuse Collection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity System</li><li>Full Vacuum System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Refuse Collection System market includes two main types: the Gravity System and the Full Vacuum System. The Gravity System relies on gravity to move waste through an underground network of pipes to a collection point, making it energy-efficient and cost-effective. In contrast, the Full Vacuum System uses suction to transport waste from collection points to a central location, allowing for flexible installations and minimizing odor and noise. Both systems offer modern, efficient solutions for urban waste management.</p></p>
