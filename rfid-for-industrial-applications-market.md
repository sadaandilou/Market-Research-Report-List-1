<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID for Industrial Applications Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency (LF)</li><li>High Frequency (HF)</li><li>Ultra High Frequency (UHF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) technology is categorized into three main frequency types for industrial applications: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra High Frequency (UHF). LF operates at 125-134 kHz, offering short read ranges, typically used for access control and animal tracking. HF operates at 13.56 MHz, enabling moderate range and is often used for inventory management and smart labels. UHF, spanning 300 MHz to 960 MHz, provides longer read ranges and higher data transfer rates, making it ideal for logistics and supply chain management.</p></p>
