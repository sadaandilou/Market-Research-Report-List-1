Single cap systems utilize one cap that incorporates an integrated disinfecting mechanism to sanitize the connector, ensuring a straightforward application. In contrast, double cap systems involve two separate caps—typically one for the connector and another for the access point—allowing for more thorough disinfection and enhanced sterility. Both systems aim to reduce infection risk in healthcare settings while maintaining ease of use.</p></p>
