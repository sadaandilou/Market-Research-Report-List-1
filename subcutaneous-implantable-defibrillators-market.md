<p><p>Subcutaneous Implantable Defibrillators (S-ICDs) are innovative devices designed for patients at risk of sudden cardiac arrest. Unlike traditional transvenous defibrillators, S-ICDs are implanted subcutaneously, minimizing the risk of infections and complications associated with leads that sit within the heart. These devices continuously monitor the heart's rhythm and provide life-saving shocks when necessary, making them a crucial tool in cardiac care.</p><p>The Subcutaneous Implantable Defibrillators Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by factors such as increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in technology, and a growing awareness of preventive cardiac solutions.
